# About WordleHint

`WordleHint` is an independent fan site dedicated to helping players enjoy and improve at Wordle. We offer unlimited free Wordle-style games in multiple word lengths (4 to 11 letters), strategy guides, and tips to sharpen your word puzzle skills.

## What WordleHint offers

We provide a comprehensive Wordle experience:

- **Unlimited free games** — Play as many Wordle puzzles as you want, in any word length from 4 to 11 letters
- **Strategy guides** — In-depth guides covering best starting words, letter frequency analysis, and advanced solving techniques
- **Word length variety** — 8 different game formats from quick 4-letter puzzles to extreme 11-letter challenges
- **No account required** — Just visit the site and start playing instantly, no download or registration needed

## How our games work

Each game randomly selects a hidden word from a curated list appropriate for the chosen word length. You have 6 attempts to guess the word, with color-coded feedback after each guess:

1. **Green** — The letter is correct and in the right position
2. **Yellow** — The letter is in the word but in a different position
3. **Gray** — The letter is not in the word at all

Use the feedback strategically to narrow down possibilities and solve the puzzle in as few guesses as possible.

## What makes WordleHint different

- **Multiple word lengths** — From 4 to 11 letters, offering something for every skill level
- **Unlimited play** — No daily limit. Play as many games as you want, anytime
- **Strategy-focused** — We don't just offer games; we help you get better with guides, tips, and starting word recommendations
- **Clean design** — No pop-ups, no autoplay videos, no fake countdown timers. Just word games and helpful content

## Content scope

This site provides free Wordle-style word games, strategy guides, and educational content about word puzzles. Our games are inspired by the original Wordle format but are independently developed.
