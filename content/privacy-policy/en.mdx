# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect on `wordlehint.info`, how advertising and analytics on this site work, and the choices you have. We are committed to keeping your experience private and transparent.

## Information we may collect

- **Analytics data** — page views, device type, browser, referral source, and country-level location
- **Advertising data** — cookies and similar identifiers used by our advertising partners to serve and measure ads (see "Advertising" below)
- **Email addresses** — if you contact us directly, or if you voluntarily subscribe to our email newsletter
- **Technical logs** — server-side logs used for security monitoring and site performance

We do not collect names, physical addresses, payment information, or any game account details.

## How we use information

- Operate and improve the website experience
- Display and measure advertising that funds the free content on this site
- Detect abuse, spam, and maintain service stability
- Respond to direct support or correction requests
- Send the newsletter you subscribed to (you can unsubscribe at any time)
- Understand aggregate usage trends (e.g., which pages are most visited)

We do not sell your personal data. Data shared with the third-party services described below is limited to what those services need to provide advertising, analytics, and email delivery.

## Advertising

We use **Google AdSense** to display ads on this site. Because of this:

- **Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites.**
- Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visits to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.
- Third-party vendors and ad networks may place and read cookies on your browser, or use web beacons or IP addresses, to collect information as a result of ad serving on this website.

**Your choices:**

- You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google Ads Settings](https://adssettings.google.com/).
- You may opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting [www.aboutads.info/choices](https://www.aboutads.info/choices).
- A list of Google's certified external ad-technology providers is available in [Google's advertising documentation](https://support.google.com/adsense/answer/9012903).

## Consent (EEA, UK, and Switzerland)

If you visit from the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you will be shown a consent message (a Google-certified consent management platform) before advertising or analytics cookies are used. Until you make a choice, advertising and analytics storage is disabled by default in these regions (Google Consent Mode).

You can change or withdraw your consent at any time by reopening the privacy message via the ad or footer options on the site, or by clearing your cookies for `wordlehint.info`, which will cause the consent message to be shown again.

## Cookies and analytics

We use **Google Analytics** to understand aggregate traffic patterns (which pages are visited, from which countries, on which devices). Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish visitors; the resulting statistics are aggregate and do not identify you personally. We may also use privacy-respecting analytics services (such as Plausible) that do not use cookies or track individual users across sites.

Cookies used on this site fall into these categories:

- **Advertising cookies** — set by Google and its partners to serve and measure ads (see "Advertising" above)
- **Analytics cookies** — used to measure aggregate site usage
- **Functional storage** — local preferences such as dark/light theme and game state, stored in your browser and never transmitted to us

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings at any time; the site's games and hints will continue to work.

## Newsletter

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we store your email address and use it only to send you the emails you signed up for. Emails are delivered through our email service provider (Resend). Every email includes an unsubscribe link, and you can also unsubscribe at any time via our [unsubscribe page](/unsubscribe). When you unsubscribe, we stop sending you emails and remove your address from the active mailing list.

## Data retention

We keep technical and analytics data only as long as reasonably required for operations and security — typically no longer than 90 days for server logs. Email correspondence is retained only to maintain context for ongoing support threads. Newsletter addresses are retained until you unsubscribe.

## Third-party links

This site links to external sources including The New York Times. Their privacy practices are governed by their own policies. We encourage you to review those policies when visiting linked sites.

## Children's privacy

`wordlehint.info` is a general-audience informational site. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13, and we do not direct interest-based advertising at children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

## Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- **Access** — request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
- **Deletion** — request that we delete your personal data
- **Correction** — request that we correct inaccurate data
- **Objection / withdrawal of consent** — object to processing or withdraw consent for personalized advertising at any time (see "Consent" above)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the site or applicable regulations change. The "Updated on" date at the top reflects the latest revision. Significant changes will be reflected on this page before they take effect.
